Contact

Contact Household Expense Tools.

Use this page for site questions, tool feedback, privacy questions, correction requests, or reports about something that is not working correctly.

Before you contact

  • No account is required to use the tools
  • No fake contact form is used on this page
  • Do not send passwords or sensitive financial details

Current contact method

Email contact

The current contact method is email. Please use it for site questions, tool feedback, privacy questions, or correction requests.

Email address

Email: householdexpensetool@gmail.com

Please include a clear subject line so the message is easier to understand.

Useful messages

What you can send

Short, specific messages are easiest to review.

  • Feedback about any household tool
  • Reports about broken links or page errors
  • Questions about privacy or browser storage
  • Suggestions for simple household expense tools
  • Requests to correct unclear or outdated site information

Message privacy

Do not send sensitive information

If you send an email, the information in your message may be used to read and respond to your request.

Please do not send passwords, bank account numbers, card details, tax documents, private family financial records, or other sensitive information. Household Expense Tools does not need those details to answer normal site questions.

Response expectation

Replies may not be immediate

Messages may be reviewed when possible, but a reply is not guaranteed. This website does not currently offer live support, phone support, or account-based customer service.

For important financial, tax, legal, investment, or accounting questions, do not rely on this contact page. Please use a qualified professional or trusted official source.
