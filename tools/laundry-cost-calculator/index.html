How it works

The calculator adds your manual per-load laundry costs, then multiplies that cost by your weekly load count. Monthly cost uses 4.345 weeks as an average month, and yearly cost uses 52 weeks.

What the result means

Cost per load shows the estimated cost for one laundry load. Weekly, monthly, and yearly totals show how that per-load cost adds up over time. Largest cost part shows which single cost input contributes the most.

Limitations

This is a simple manual-cost estimate. It does not calculate electricity usage from wattage, machine efficiency, utility rates, taxes, or laundry product size. It also does not convert currencies.

This tool is for personal estimation only and does not provide financial, tax, legal, investment, or accounting advice.
