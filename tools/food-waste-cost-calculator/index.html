How it works

The calculator divides wasted quantity by total quantity to estimate the wasted percentage. It then applies that percentage to the total cost to estimate the wasted value.

What the result means

Estimated wasted value is the approximate cost of the wasted portion. Estimated used value is the part of the total cost that was not wasted.

Limitations

This is a simple personal estimate only. It assumes the wasted quantity has the same average cost per unit as the total quantity bought. It does not convert currencies or units.
