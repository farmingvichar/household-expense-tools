How it works

The calculator compares final pack price against total sheets and ply-adjusted sheets. It is useful when store labels show confusing roll names or different unit prices.

What the result means

Cost per 100 sheets shows a simple sheet-based comparison. Cost per 100 ply-sheets adjusts for paper layers, so it is usually the better value check when comparing different ply counts.

Limitations

This is a simple household shopping estimate. It does not judge softness, strength, brand quality, eco score, subscription savings, taxes, shipping, or personal usage needs. It does not convert currencies.
