    How to use the Daily Expense Tracker

    Use the tracker for simple daily household expense logging in your browser.

    1. Add the expense details

    Enter a short expense name, add the amount as a number without any currency symbol, choose a category, and select the date.

    2. Save the entry

    Select Add expense to save the entry locally in this browser on this device.

    3. Review and manage

    Review today’s total, this month’s total, and your saved entries. Delete single entries or clear all expenses when needed.

    What this tracker calculates

    This tool calculates the total amount added for the current browser date, the total amount added for the current month, and the total number of saved entries.

    It is designed for simple household tracking only. It does not provide budgeting advice, financial advice, tax advice, legal advice, or account-level reporting.

    Privacy and localStorage note

    The tracker uses your browser’s localStorage to remember the expenses you enter. This means your data stays on the current device and browser unless you clear it.

    Because there is no account system or backend, your saved entries will not automatically sync across devices or browsers.

    FAQ

    Common questions about this browser-based expense tracker.

    Is this daily expense tracker free to use?

    Yes. The Daily Expense Tracker is free to use in your browser and does not require login or account registration.

    Where are my expense entries saved?

    Your entries are saved only in localStorage in the browser on your current device. They are not sent to a server.

    Can I delete saved expenses?

    Yes. You can delete a single expense from the list or clear all saved expenses after confirmation.

    Does this tool give financial advice?

    No. This tool is only a simple household expense tracking utility and should not be treated as financial, tax, or legal advice.
