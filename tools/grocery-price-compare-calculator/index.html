How it works

The calculator divides each item’s total price by its quantity to estimate price per selected unit. It then compares both unit prices and shows the lower-cost option.

What the result means

The better value result points to the item with the lower price per selected unit. The difference per unit shows how much the unit prices differ.

Limitations

This is a simple personal estimate only. It does not compare quality, freshness, delivery fees, discounts, taxes, or store differences. It does not convert units or currencies.

This tool does not provide financial, tax, legal, investment, or accounting advice.
